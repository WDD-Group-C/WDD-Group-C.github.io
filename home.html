Impact of Plastic Pollution on Marine Life and Ecosystems

Plastic pollution severely impacts marine life and ecosystems, causing harm to wildlife, disrupting habitats, and contributing to the degradation of ocean health and biodiversity.

Coral Reef Degradation and Restoration Efforts

Coral reef degradation threatens marine ecosystems globally. Restoration efforts focus on rebuilding and preserving these vital habitats to ensure the survival of diverse marine species and maintain ocean health.

Overfishing and Sustainable Fisheries Management

Overfishing depletes fish populations and harms marine ecosystems. Sustainable fisheries management aims to balance fishery activities with conservation efforts to ensure long-term viability and health of fish stocks.

Marine Protected Areas in Biodiversity Conservation

Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) are designated zones aimed at conserving marine ecosystems and biodiversity, ensuring sustainable use of marine resources, and protecting habitats and species from human activities.
