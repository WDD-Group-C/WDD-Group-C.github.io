Reduce Marine Pollution

Marine pollution is the combination of chemicals and trash humans make,
which are blown or thrown into the ocean. While that is a solution to get rid of trash on land,
it threatens the lives of marine life and therefore, needs to be reduced.
To fulfill the act, the following steps could be taken;

  • Stop the usage of plastic bags (Use reusable bags and bottles instead)
  • Get together as a community to clean rivers and beaches
  • Avoid littering
  • Proper disposal of trash
