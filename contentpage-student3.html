Coral reefs are vital ecosystems that support a diverse array of marine life and play a crucial role in sustaining our planet's health. However, these intricate underwater structures face severe threats from human activities, climate change, and environmental degradation. As coral reefs decline, so too does their ability to provide essential benefits to both marine life and human communities worldwide.
